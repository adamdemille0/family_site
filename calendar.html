Adding Events

Events are managed directly in Google Calendar — no need to edit any files. Anyone with access to the DeMille Family calendar can add, edit, or remove events and they’ll appear here automatically. To share the calendar with another family member, open Google Calendar, click the three dots next to the calendar name, choose Settings & sharing, and add their Gmail address.
