Recipe February 5, 2026

Aunt Carol’s Famous Green Chili — Recipe Inside!

After decades of requests, Aunt Carol has finally written down her legendary green chili recipe! Serves 8–10 and freezes beautifully.

Ingredients: 2 lbs pork shoulder, 8 Hatch green chiles (roasted & peeled), 1 large onion, 4 garlic cloves, 2 cups chicken broth, 1 tsp cumin, salt & pepper to taste.
Method: Brown pork in batches. Sauté onion and garlic, add pork, chiles, broth, and spices. Simmer low and slow for 2–3 hours until pork is fall-apart tender. Serve with warm flour tortillas. Carol’s tip: roast the chiles yourself for the best flavor!

— Shared by Aunt Carol
